Our Collection


Welcome to Killing Fashion Collaborations, the ultimate destination for fashionable collaborative footwear. Our company specializes in curating and selling a wide range of shoes featuring exciting collaborations between top designers and brands.


At Killing Fashion Collaborations, we believe that fashion can be unique and empowering. Our collection includes limited edition sneakers, boots, and sandals that are sure to showcase your individuality and charm.


Whether you're looking for the latest Nike x Off-White collaboration or an exclusive Adidas x Pharrell Williams release, you'll find it here at Killing Fashion Collaborations. We take pride in offering the best selection of footwear from around the world, all in one convenient place.

Nike: DUNK LOW

The Nike Dunk Low is a classic low-cut sneaker with a padded tongue and collar for comfort, durable leather and suede upper, and iconic Nike swoosh branding.

Nike: Air Force

The Air Force is a classic high-top sneaker from Nike with a leather upper, perforated toe box, padded collar, and chunky sole for a timeless, versatile look.

Nike: Air Jordan

The Air Jordan is a legendary basketball sneaker from Nike, designed in collaboration with Michael Jordan. Featuring a sleek silhouette, durable leather upper, and iconic Jumpman logo, the Air Jordan is a true classic.

Dior x Nike Air Jordan 1

The Dior x Nike Air Jordan 1 is a luxury sneaker collaboration that combines the iconic Air Jordan 1 silhouette with Dior's high-end fashion aesthetic. Featuring a premium leather upper, embroidered Swoosh, and Dior branding, this shoe is a rare and coveted collector's item.
